About CueTeddy

Learn a bit more about how CueTeddy came about.

What is CueTeddy?

CueTeddy is a soft, cuddly bear that interacts in a personal and meaningful manner with an individual who has mild to moderate dementia. CueTeddy can decrease the isolation that a person suffering from memory loss experiences in a nursing home or family home setting.

What can CueTeddy do?

CueTeddy may help improve long term memory, improve conversation, and reduce fear and anxiety for the user. CueTeddy can help your loved one recall a safe and happy time from earlier days. Why not give your loved one the device that engages them in a meaningful manner?
